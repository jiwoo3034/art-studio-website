About Gitelle

At Gitelle Studio, we believe art transcends boundaries. Founded in 2020, our creative sanctuary embraces both traditional craftsmanship and bold experimentation.

Our name "Gitelle," derived from the ancient word for "divine wings," reflects our commitment to elevating artistic expression beyond the ordinary. Here, painters and sculptors alike find space to explore, create, and soar.

Led by acclaimed artist Eliza Merritt, our studio has become a vibrant hub for Kitchener's diverse artistic community. We provide not just instruction, but inspiration—fostering an environment where technique meets imagination.

Whether you're a seasoned artist refining your craft or a curious beginner taking your first creative steps, Gitelle offers you the guidance, tools, and community to transform your artistic vision into reality.

Join us in our light-filled studio space where every brushstroke and sculpted form tells a story, and where your artistic journey can truly take flight.
